== Description ==

Vite Coupon simplifies coupon management for WooCommerce stores. Whether you want to offer discounts, free products, or buy-one-get-one (BOGO) deals, this plugin has you covered. With an intuitive interface, you can create and manage coupons effortlessly, enhancing your marketing efforts and increasing customer satisfaction.

Key features of Vite Coupon include:

- **Multiple Coupon Types:** Choose from various coupon types, including discount percentage, fixed discount, product discount, free product, and BOGO product, to suit your promotional needs.

- **Easy Management:** Create, edit, and delete coupons with ease. The plugin provides a user-friendly interface for efficient coupon management.

- **Integration with VitePOS:** Seamlessly integrate Vite Coupon with VitePOS, ensuring a smooth experience for your point-of-sale transactions.

- **Customizable Settings:** Tailor coupon settings to match your business requirements. Set usage limits, expiration dates, minimum purchase amounts, and more.

- **Track Performance:** Monitor the performance of your coupons with built-in tracking features. Gain insights into usage statistics and adjust your marketing strategies accordingly.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `vite-coupon` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to the WooCommerce settings and configure the Vite Coupon options according to your preferences.
4. Start creating coupons and boosting your sales!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

### Is Vite Coupon compatible with other WooCommerce extensions?

Yes, Vite Coupon is designed to work seamlessly with WooCommerce and integrates well with various extensions.

### Can I customize the appearance of coupons?

While Vite Coupon focuses on coupon functionality, you can use custom CSS to adjust the appearance of coupons on your website.

### How can I track the performance of my coupons?

Vite Coupon provides built-in tracking features to monitor coupon usage and performance. You can access this data within the plugin's settings.
